Who we are

We, Global Insights KPO offer a comprehensive portfolio of outsourcing solutions for various sizes of business organisations. Our clients get best quality service with access to the latest skills and technologies in a very efficient manner.




We are, emerging KPO having a specialized team of accounting, management consultancy and auditing services. Our team consists of certified public accountants, MBA professionals, experienced accountants, semi qualified and other skilled people.




Our Objective is to get insights into the global need of outsourcing and provide quality services in a cost efficient manner to achieve higher levels of satisfaction for our Clients.

Why Global Insights KPO

  • - Having the experience of more than 5 years in the finance industry along with the experts from various global firms, we commit to provide quality services to our clients.
  • - As we are into the profession of trust, we believe that the safety and security of data is of utmost importance. Hence, we are using the latest technologies to ensure that the client's data is kept confidential and secure.
  • - Apart from traditional accounting services, our firm believes in providing more insights about the financial health of your business like various KPIs & ratio analysis so that the business owners can get to know the overall financial picture of the business & thereby becoming a valuable partner in your business success.
  • - Our firm is committed to using the latest technology and innovative approaches of accounting. As a result, it streamlines processes, increases accuracy, and provides real-time data that can help our clients to make informed business decisions.
  • - We are committed to provide excellent customer service, being responsive to clients' needs, and maintaining open and clear communication at all times.
  • - Maintaining the same level of trust & confidence for all our new clients as we are having for our current 40+ happy Clients is our top most priority.
  • - We believe in consistency. Hence, we take continuous feedback from our clients on a regular basis so as to maintain a higher level of satisfaction from our Clients.
