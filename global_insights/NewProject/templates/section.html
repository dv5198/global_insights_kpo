Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to help individuals and businesses to manage their finances effectively.

Accounting/Bookkeeping

Accurate and quality accounting is the backbone of any business. To keep business managers/individuals free from hustles of maintenance of books, we are here for you by providing cost-effective and efficient services.

Year End Finalization

Include correct & timely compilation of the balance sheet & the profit and loss statement. We make sure that accounts are logically verified and reconciled in compliance with the requirements of any applicable accounting standards.

Payroll Advisory

Payrolling takes time and requires precision, but we offer affordable and effective solutions for all payroll requirements, including weekly and monthly payroll, payslip preparation, and headcount reconciliation.

Management Consultancy

MIS report development process involves providing quarterly/annual financial reports with in-depth insights, financial analysis, & KPIs to decision-making, creating SOPsfor corporate operations, timely creation of paperwork & reports to banks & government agencies etc..

Pre-audit Review of Books

Verify all transactions are accurately documented, implement cut off processes, follow year-end processes, identify revenue leaks, scrutinize transactions involving delayed revenue, unbilled revenue, prepayments, & similar items, & complete & supply a draught set of financial statements.

Account Receivable Management

Include managing customer billing, reviewing credit terms, making analysis of opportunity costs, debtor turnover ratios, preparing customer ageing reports, suggesting incentive & discount policies, providing policies for doubtful debts.
